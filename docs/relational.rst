Relational
===========

The Gretel Trainer Relational sub-package provides interfaces for processing
multitple tables from realational databases and datawarehouses. You may
utilize a `Connector` to automaticaly connect to support databases or
provide your own CSVs and define relationships manually.

Please see https://docs.gretel.ai/reference/relational to get started.
